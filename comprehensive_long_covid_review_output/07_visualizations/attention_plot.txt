Key Findings:
• Long COVID patients show significant attention deficits (g = -0.97)
• Effect sizes are consistent across studies (I² = 39.8%, moderate heterogeneity)
• Largest deficits observed by Lauren et al. (g = -1.12)
• Smallest deficits observed by Jaywant et al. (g = -0.85)
